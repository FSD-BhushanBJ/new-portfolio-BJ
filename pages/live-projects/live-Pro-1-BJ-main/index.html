About Us


Sai Transformation is Katol based metal manufacturer fabrication company known for roofing sheds, metal and school furniture’s, gates & window grills. A trusted branch of Sai Fabrication & Gas Welding (Est: 1998).


We offer a wide range of metal fabrication services, including Gates,,Window-Grills,Staircases,Industry Shed & Roofing and more. Our team possesses the knowledge and skills to handle any project from standard designs to unique,custom-build structures.
